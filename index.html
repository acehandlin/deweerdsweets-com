Our Sweet Story

DeWeerd Sweets began as a family tradition of sharing homemade treats with friends and neighbors. What started in a small kitchen has grown into a beloved local confectionery, but our commitment to quality and personal touch remains the same.


Every sweet we create is made with carefully selected ingredients, time-honored techniques, and most importantly, love. We believe that life's sweetest moments deserve the perfect treat to match, and we're honored to be part of your celebrations.


From our family to yours, we invite you to experience the magic of DeWeerd Sweets.
